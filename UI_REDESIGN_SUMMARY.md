### **Typography Scale**
- **App Title**: 34px, Bold - Main headings
- **App Subtitle**: 22px, Semibold - Section headings  
- **App Body**: 17px, Regular - Body text
- **App Caption**: 15px, Medium - Small text

- `input-apple` - Form inputs
- `nav-apple` - Navigation bars
- `sidebar-apple` - Sidebar components
